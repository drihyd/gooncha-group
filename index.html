Established in 2018, we strive to be a pioneer by not just being a participant but a contributor to every industry we set foot in, with a strong focus to deliver excellence at any given point. Gooncha's ethos is centered around the preservation of its founding parents' (Shrimati Kusum and Shri Inder Mohan Kapur) vision and principles, which fortify the foundations of our work ethic.

The name 'Gooncha', means a blooming flower bud in Persian language. With that in mind, we, at Goon cha believe that as tremendously we may contribute to the economy moving forward, we will still have ample place to blossom further.

Our logo bears the insignia of a chess knight. The chess knight is a noble piece on the chessboard that has the ability to jump over other objects. It acts in the best interests of the queen. We strongly intend on making that behavioral pattern our route to success, preserving our lineage, yet being adaptable to the ever-changing landscape of the 21st century.
